World's All Time Top 3 Movies :

"The Godfather" (1972) directed by Francis Ford Coppola:

This iconic crime drama explores the Corleone family's rise and fall within the mafia. With its exceptional performances, gripping storyline, and expertly crafted scenes, it is often considered one of the greatest films ever made.

"Citizen Kane" (1941) directed by Orson Welles:

This groundbreaking masterpiece follows the life of Charles Foster Kane, a wealthy newspaper magnate, and examines themes of power, corruption, and the complexities of human nature. It is praised for its innovative storytelling techniques and has been highly influential in the world of cinema.

"The Shawshank Redemption" (1994) directed by Frank Darabont:

Based on Stephen King's novella, this emotionally powerful drama tells the story of Andy Dufresne, a man who finds hope and redemption within the confines of Shawshank State Penitentiary. It has resonated with audiences worldwide and is celebrated for its stellar performances, poignant themes, and a gripping narrative.
