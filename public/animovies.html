World's All Time Top 3 Animated Movies :

"Spirited Away" (2001) directed by Hayao Miyazaki :

This enchanting and visually stunning Japanese animated film follows the story of a young girl named Chihiro, who discovers a magical world filled with spirits. With its imaginative storytelling, breathtaking animation, and deep thematic exploration, it has garnered universal acclaim and is often regarded as one of the greatest animated films of all time.

"Toy Story" (1995) directed by John Lasseter :

The first feature-length film produced by Pixar Animation Studios, "Toy Story" revolutionized the animation industry. This heartwarming and humorous tale follows a group of toys who come to life when humans aren't around. It introduced groundbreaking CGI animation and ushered in a new era of storytelling in animation.

"The Lion King" (1994) directed by Roger Allers and Rob Minkoff :

This beloved Disney classic tells the story of a young lion named Simba, who must reclaim his rightful place as king after the murder of his father. With its unforgettable characters, memorable songs, and a powerful coming-of-age narrative, "The Lion King" has left an indelible mark on animation and is cherished by audiences of all ages.
